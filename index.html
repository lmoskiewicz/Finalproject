National Park Service Guide and Information

This Guide can be used to help plan a trip to any National Park Location. By using the yearly visitation trend data, you can see which areas are trending up and which areas are trending down. You do not want to vacation where it is crowded? The guide can help you decide on a National Park Area with little visitation. Or maybe you want to plan a bucket list trip but are not quite sure where to go? By choosing an area with millions of visiters every year, you will be planning the trip of a lifetime.

Designation and Pricing Information

This map will help you plan your trip depending on your interests and budget. The National Park system has many options that include National Parks, Historical Sites, National Monuments, National Battlefields, National Seashores, and Recreation Areas. This map is interactive by clicking on the icons you can see the State and Pricing Information to help plan a trip that is within your budget.
